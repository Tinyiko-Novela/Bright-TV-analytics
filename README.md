# Bright-TV-analytics
Bright TV is one of the broadcasting satellite services in South Africa. 
It provides television and streaming services to thousands of subscribers.
For this finanancial year, the CEO has an objective to grow the subscription base for the company.
To meet this objective, the CEO needs to understand the customer base that we have, therefore, he has tasked the data analyst of the
company with using historical data to better understand customer Behavior. 
The data analyst has to extract insights to answer the following 
questions:
1. What type of factors influence consumption?
2. Content recommendations for low consumption days
3. Initiatives to increase subscription base
   
   -----FINDINGS--------
-Usage trends
Viewrship increased from January 2016 to March 2016.
Fridays and Saturdays have the highest viewership counts.
Compared to the other time slots, afternoons have the highest viewership times
About 73% of the viewers change channels in less than 10 minutes, only 11% watch the shows for longer than an hour

1. What type of factors influence consumption?
Age, gender and race were found to be influencers viewership.
From the visualization it was deduced that males, adults(29-64) and black people have high viewership counts.
   
2. Content recommendations for low consumption days
Males contribute to 87% of the viewers by gender. According to the data, they prefer sports and music. Curate the content to include interesting games and music on low consumption days
Ages 30 and 64 have high counts of viewership, shows that are preferred by adults should be played on low viewership days.
The afternoon time slot( 12pm-6pm) has a high count of viewers, schedule TV shows and hit movies, advertise them prior to get viewership on low consumption days.

3. Initiatives to increase subscription base
Use social media advertising
Put interesting shows on less busy days to boost viewership
Use ML models to study customer preferences and send recommendations via email
Have discounts for family subscriptions to encourage current customers to join with their families
Survey customers to find out more about their preferences, areas of improvement for Bright TV and how to curate our services to their liking
